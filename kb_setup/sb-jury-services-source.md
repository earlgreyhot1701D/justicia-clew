## Reporting Instructions

If you have received a Trial Jury Summons, examine the entire document carefully for the following information:

* Summons Date
* Jury Group Number
* Juror ID Number
* 24 hour information phone line

You will need to check the Superior Court's online jury portal or call the court's automated phone line at 877-544-5094 after 5:30 PM the evening prior to your summons date for the reporting instructions for your group. You will need to have your Juror ID number available.

## Lost or Didn't Receive Your Summons?

If a juror lost or didn't receive their summons or doesn't have access to their Juror ID number, contact Jury Services directly. Office hours are 8 a.m. to 3 p.m., Monday through Friday, excluding holidays.
North County: (805) 614-6464; South County: (805) 882-4530.

## Check Jury Reporting Status

You must access the Superior Court's online jury portal or call the court's automated phone line at 877-544-5094 to check for your status as directed during your on-call period to determine if you are required to report for service.

Please have your summons handy when calling. You will need to enter your specific Juror ID Number to access your record.

You will be placed on call for approximately five (5) court days. If directed to report for jury service you MUST bring your summons with you when you report and you MUST be on time. If you are not directed to report for service during your on-call period, you will be released as instructed on the recorded message or the court's website.

## Juror Parking

**Santa Barbara Courthouse:**
Limited juror parking is available at 1021 Santa Barbara Street (located between Figueroa & Carrillo Streets). Parking is extremely limited and is first-come first-served. For reporting prospective jurors, please show your summons to the parking attendant to gain entry to the lot. The jury parking lot opens at 7:30am. Parking for a fee is also available in the city parking lot at the corner of Anacapa and Figueroa streets. DO NOT PARK in any timed zone spaces as your car may be ticketed. Parking tickets will not be validated.

**Santa Maria Courthouse:**
There is limited juror parking at the courthouse. It is recommended to park in the Town Center mall parking lot on Cook Street and walk to the Jury Assembly Building F located in the rear of the courthouse complex. If driving from US 101 North, exit at Stowell Road in the right hand lane and head west on Stowell. Proceed approximately one mile to Miller Street. Turn right on Miller Street and proceed approximately one mile. Turn left on Cook Street. If driving from Lompoc, take CA-135/Broadway north to Santa Maria. Turn right on Cook Street.

**Lompoc Courthouse:**
Parking is available in the Civic Center parking lot and in the County lot located behind the courthouse. If driving from Santa Maria, proceed south on Broadway/CA-135 approximately 4.5 miles south of Orcutt. Merge right onto Highway 1 towards Vandenberg Space Force Base. Follow Highway 1 for 6 miles and turn left at Vandenberg Space Force Base main gate. Continue 6.5 miles and merge right onto H Street. Follow H Street south through Lompoc to Ocean Avenue, approximately 2.5 miles. Turn left onto Ocean Avenue to D Street. Turn right onto D Street, into the Civic Center parking lot and in the County lot behind the courthouse.
